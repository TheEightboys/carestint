# **App Name**: CareStint

## Core Features:

- OTP Login: Enable employers and professionals to log in using one-time passwords sent to their phones.
- Stint Posting and Application: Allow employers to post stints with details such as profession, shift type, and pay rate, and allow professionals to apply or bid on these stints.
- Automated KYC/License Verification: Automate the verification of employer and professional credentials, including license checks and document quality assessment.
- Automated Payouts: Automatically calculate and process payments to professionals after the dispute window closes, accounting for platform fees and M-Pesa costs.
- Risk Scoring: Calculate and display risk scores for clinics and professionals based on factors like cancellation rates, disputes, and no-shows.
- Audit Logging: Record all significant SuperAdmin actions and automated rules to provide a comprehensive audit trail.
- AI-Powered Suggestion: Employ a tool that analyzes past dispute cases (same region, role, issue type) and suggests the correct resolution. This helps speed up SuperAdmin action.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Navy blue (#2E4756), reflecting trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Dark navy (#1A2A33) to provide a comfortable and focused user experience, complementing the primary color.
- Accent color: Teal (#45B69C), to highlight key interactive elements, contrasting against the darker palette.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, for a techy feel, particularly in headings and CTAs.
- Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif, to ensure high readability and modern look for paragraphs and descriptions.
- Use clean, simple icons with a line-art style in white or teal to maintain consistency and legibility on the dark background.
- Use subtle animations for transitions and feedback, such as smooth accordion height changes and loading indicators.
